# Profile (copy to PROFILE.md and fill in)

The one file that makes the assistant yours. Copy this to `PROFILE.md` (gitignored) and replace the prompts. Keep it self-contained so you can drop it on any machine.

## Who I am
- Name, what you do, your level (e.g. beginner vs experienced engineer).
- Your projects/ventures the assistant should know about (one line each).
- Your OS and shell, and any environment quirks to watch for.

## How I communicate, and how you should
- Tone you want (e.g. concise and direct, dry humor, no sycophancy).
- How to handle disagreement (e.g. tell me plainly when I'm wrong; fact-check me when I hedge).
- Anything to always do (e.g. flag costs up front; default to free/local).

## How to do work for me
- Command style (e.g. copy-paste-ready PowerShell; state what success looks like).
- Pace (e.g. one step at a time).
- What needs confirmation vs. what to just do.
- Code conventions (e.g. no comments unless asked; edit existing files over new ones).

## My writing voice (when drafting AS me)
- General voice in one line (e.g. plain, direct, evidence-first; no em dashes; no filler).
- Email/message voice (length, greeting, sign-off, structure).
- Formal/long-form voice (structure, how you open and close).
- What your voice is NOT.
